Club profile / 球會簡介

聲威唔係靠架構，而係靠一班人逢星期三踢波維繫

球會簡介

原頁形容聲威係一個好非正式嘅球會：冇會長、冇文書、冇幹事，主要由會員同一個類似財政嘅人處理球會錢銀。球會「成年都唔開一次會」，重點係會員定期約出嚟踢波。

The archived profile describes Sing Wai as an informal club: no president, secretary or committee, only members and a treasurer-like person handling the money. It was less about administration and more about people regularly meeting to play football.

球會精神

可讀到嘅原文語氣好有朋友味：唔係一個制度化組織，而係一班人用「義氣」、友情同星期三踢波去維持球會生命。

The tone is friendly and proudly informal: a club held together by loyalty, friendship and the rhythm of Wednesday football.

聲威近況 / Club updates

1998-05-09

出戰總會盃足球賽

原頁記錄九八年五月九日（星期六）聲威將會出戰總會盃足球賽；賽事以分組單循環進行，亦寫到「是日聲威首次出戰力爭佳績」。

The archive says Sing Wai would play in a cup competition on Saturday, 9 May 1998, using a group round-robin format. It also notes the team was making its first appearance and aiming for a good result.

1998 archive

其他杯賽更新

Search snippets also show further tournament updates, including items around cup knockout rounds such as last-16, quarter-final, semi-final and final reports. Because the original HTML times out and the indexed Chinese is partly garbled, this remake preserves the readable structure and links back to the source.

Records / 球會戰績

原頁列出嘅戰績類別

原站「球會戰績」以年份列出賽事成績。可讀到嘅項目包括小型足球總會邀請賽、反斗盃七人足球賽、聲威四角足球邀請賽、青少年足球賽等，名次包括冠軍、亞軍、高級組亞軍等。

The “records” section listed results by year. Readable entries include invitational small-sided football, the Fandou seven-a-side cup, Sing Wai four-team invitation matches and youth football competitions, with positions such as champion, runner-up and senior-group runner-up.
