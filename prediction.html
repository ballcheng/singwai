估波挑戰賽主頁

聲威估波挑戰賽

經過咁多週嘅賽事，冠亞季軍已經誕生，現在正式宣布，第二屆聲威估波挑戰賽總成績：

After many weeks of matches, the champion, runner-up and third place of the second Sing Wai Prediction Challenge were confirmed.

🏆
冠軍 子凌 Champion · Tsz Ling
🥈
亞軍 阿明 Runner-up · Ah Ming
🥉
季軍 阿倫 Third place · Ah Lun

子凌喺每週賽事估波常有佳作，其中憑一輪幾週得十五分嘅攻勢遙遙領先，拋離最接近對手十幾分有多，亦憑這段優勢帶返終點，以八十分勇奪冠軍。

Tsz Ling often performed strongly in the weekly match predictions. A run of several weeks scoring fifteen points gave him a commanding lead, and he carried that advantage to the finish, winning the title with eighty points.

阿明亦係每週賽事偶有佳作，而憑聯賽冠軍、神射手等估波賽事憑憑得手，終以七十分勇奪亞軍。

Ah Ming also had some strong weekly results, and his correct picks in categories such as league champion and top scorer helped him finish second with seventy points.

至於季軍就爭持激烈，終於阿倫以估中降班隊及神射手以一分之微壓到阿強及阿榮勇奪季軍。

The battle for third place was very close. Ah Lun eventually edged past Ah Keung and Ah Wing by a single point, helped by correct picks for relegated teams and top scorer.
