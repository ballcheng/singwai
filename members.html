陳X強

花名：牙簽仔

中前場

Slim as the nickname suggests, but highly skilful, determined and a good finisher. A key midfield/attacking figure, though stamina is a slight weakness.

Very fast, technically outstanding, unpredictable in one-on-one situations, and with a strong scoring rate.

Elegant on the ball, calm under pressure, accurate in passing and one of the core players. Has returned after a serious injury.

A forward and super-sub. Not the strongest overall, but determined, opportunistic, and somehow known for a golden left foot despite being right-footed.
